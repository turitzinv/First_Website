My history with Holmdel Park

My first time discovering Holmdel Park was during an Eight Grade Cross Country competition. It was a rainy day and one of the toughest courses I ever ran. This park is notorious for its hills so if you ever felt you were catching your breathe, a new hill to climb appeared. Over time I ran at this park in High School for Cross Country, and the course itself was longer than what I did in Middle School. Holmdel Park slowly because a place I hated, to a place I loved. When I stopped running competitively, I enjoyed the scenery and the course. When I competed in a 24 hour obstacle course (maybe a link here?), all my training was done at Holmdel Park.
